# amazon-copurhcase-network
The primary objective of this study is to examine the structural characteristics of the product co-purchase network on Amazon. The study investigates whether products cluster within their own categories or form cross-category connections. Additionally, the study aims to identify “bridge” categories that connect different product types and to understand the network’s degree distribution and community structures. This analysis helps us understand consumer behavior on e-commerce platforms and the fundamental dynamics of product recommendation systems.
